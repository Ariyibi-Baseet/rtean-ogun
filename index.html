Chairman's Address

ISE YA!
This is the official website of the Ogun State Branch of Road Transport Employers Association of Nigeria (RTEAN) and I specifically welcome you on board. Since 1978, we provide efficient transportation services to the public, both privately and commercially while placing premium on their safety and comfort and our operation is duly backed by Law. Administratively and in terms of operations, we are well structured to deliver on our mandate as the foremost transportation Union in Ogun State and indeed Nigeria. The first criteria for joining us is the ownership of a vehicle, the reason we are never involved in hooliganism or any act that can endanger the lives and property of the people, because as they say, 'He that live in glass house don't throw stones ". This explains the reason for the mutual relationship that we have enjoyed with all the State's Chief Executives and their governments, including the present Governor and government led by Prince Dapo Abiodun, MFR, CON This platform was created to provide you necessary information about us, as well as our operations. However, should you require any information that is not available here, kindly reach out to us through any of the available contacts. As I leave you to browse through, please be informed that I appreciate your visit.
Omo Ogun, Ise Ya!
UP Road. Super! UP Road. Maturity!! UP UP Road. Unity and Progress
Most Sincerely Yours,
Alhaji Titilayo Akibu
a.k.a Efele Chairman,
RTEAN, Ogun State
27 April, 2024.
